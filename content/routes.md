Most rides start at **MIT Student Center** or **Porter Square Dunkin' Donuts**. Rides are posted to our mailing list year-round — subscribe to see the weekly schedule. All routes are also posted on our [Strava club](https://www.strava.com/clubs/mit-cycling-club).

Check out our friends at **[Urban Dirt](https://www.urbandirt.org/)** for mixed terrain routes in our backyard — Multi-meadow is a club favorite and great for mountain, CX, and gravel bikes.
