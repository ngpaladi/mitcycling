Membership

How to Join

Getting started takes just a few steps. No prior racing experience required.

Join MIT Club Sports

Register through MIT Club Sports (DoSportsEasy). Sign any required waivers there. This step is required for all MIT club sport members.

Pay Membership Dues

Pay dues through MIT's payment portal. Choose the tier that fits your racing plans for the season. If costs are a barrier, contact us — we want everyone to be able to participate.

Get a USA Cycling License

Racing in ECCC events requires a USA Cycling collegiate license. One-day licenses are available for occasional racers.

Show Up and Ride

Join a group ride, come to a team meeting, or just email us at {{ .Site.Params.email }}. We're a friendly crew — come say hi.
